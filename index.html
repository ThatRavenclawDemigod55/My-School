Badminton School Campus

The Rose Gardens

located by the library and sixth-form center

The Rose Gardens are a beautiful addition to the school. The Rose Gardens are maintained by our excellent gardening team, who do an excellent job of making sure these roses are always in tip-top shape! With amazing flowers that bloom and flourish every year, it is an incredible spot for students and teachers alike to sit and relax with friends or colleagues after a long day of hard work.

Northcote

located by the dining hall and school house

This is the most iconic building of Badminton School! It is where our headmistress, Mrs. Tear, has her office. It is also where the most important meetings are held with school governers.

The Sports Hall

located by the car park, just as you come in

This Sports Hall was only built in 2019, but it is our pride and joy. Many other school who come to compete with us, marvel at the wonder of this Sports Hall. Inside, there is a large hall where we do our sports, but also, many important functions are held here, such as speeches from our headmistress, and assemblies. There is also a fitness gym and a dance studio.
